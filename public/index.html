Frequently asked questions.

Which plan is right for me?

If you’re an individual account that’s less than one month old on Instagram, we reccomend you start with the a Starter account. If you’re a business, brand, or influencer, the Advanced plan is best suited for your account. For businesses or brands looking for that extra white glove experience, we reccomend Pro.

Is this safe?

Your account security is our top priority. instagrow.ai is designed with multiple layers of protection across a distributed, reliable infrastructure. Our systems run daily tests against rate limits to keep your account safe. We are also an Instagram Graph API Partner which requires our compliance with their limits.

What’s the cancellation policy?

You can cancel your account at any time. There’s no cancellation fees. If you’d like to cancel your account, you can do so from your dashboard or contact us through support chat. If you’d like to cancel your next cycle, we require 24 hours notice.

How much growth can I expect?

Growth numbers depend largely on how good your page is. The better your content, and the more often you post, the better your results with instagrow.ai will be. Accross all our users, the median growth per 30 days on the Starter Plan is 3K Followers and 8.9K Followers with Advanced.

Why do I need this?

With so much competition on Instagram, it is incredibly difficult to stand out without spending thousands of dollars on ads. instagrow.ai offers an all in one tool that creates the returns of ad spend at a low price. Get exposure to your target audience, drive traffic to your site and grow.

What’s the refund policy?

You’re billed immediately after creating your account, but we offer descretionary refunds on a case-by-case basis. If you’re unsatisfied with the service, please contact our support and we will help you figure out next steps — whether this is a refund or cancellation of your account.

How does it work?

Unlike any other platform, we don’t sell you fake followers. After spending years developing specialized marketing techniques to help you see real, targeted results, our service is completely hands free and requires absolutely no work from your end. Just click the start button and watch your account grow.

How do I upgrade my account?

Our support team will gladly assist you in upgrading your account to a higher level of service. Just contact them through the live chat and they’ll get you setup!
